ABOUT

The Reulinency International School earlier known as Reuelience Public school was established on 2 August 2007. It began in one big hall.Reulinency joined hands with Nathanian school which was big run in the present lawn area. It began as a primary school with approximately 600 children. The hall was divided into two where both LKG and UKG studnets were accommodated. One faciliatator and one staff helped to run the school. Today Reulinency International is leading chain of school under the auspices of Educational Society. It is administered by managing committee set up in accordance with Delhi School Education Act 1973.
